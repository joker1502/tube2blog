Welcome to Tube2Blog! If you've ever wished you could turn your YouTube videos into blog posts without spending hours transcribing and formatting, you're in the right place.

## What Is Tube2Blog?

Tube2Blog is a tool that takes any public YouTube video URL and transforms it into a fully structured, SEO-optimized blog post. It handles the entire workflow:

- **Transcript extraction** — Pulls captions automatically
- **SEO metadata generation** — Titles, descriptions, tags, and Schema markup
- **Thumbnail selection** — Pulls the video thumbnail for use as a featured image
- **Markdown export** — Clean, formatted output for any CMS

The best part? The free tier works without any API keys. Just paste a URL and go.

## Why We Built It

As content creators ourselves, we noticed a persistent problem: video content and written content live in separate silos. A YouTuber spends hours on a video, publishes it, and gets traffic for 48 hours. Then the video sits in the archive, gradually losing relevance.

Meanwhile, that same content could be driving search traffic for years as a blog post. But manual transcription and rewriting takes 2-3 hours per video — time most creators don't have.

We built Tube2Blog to bridge that gap. One click turns video content into text content, preserving the value and making it discoverable through search engines.

## How It Works

1. **Paste a YouTube link** — Any public video works. The transcript is extracted automatically.
2. **Review your SEO draft** — Title, description, tags, thumbnails, and FAQ Schema are generated from your content.
3. **Edit, copy, and publish** — Fine-tune in the Markdown editor with live preview. Copy the result for any CMS.

The entire process takes **under a minute** for the automated parts. Editing time depends on how much you want to customize.
